About

Now days we take soil to be a part of farming and something that farming is nothing without. Over the past year I stadies an alternate way to farm crops that does not use soil, instead uses water. Its called Hydroponic


Benefits

    - More efficent use of space
    - Wastes less water
    - Higher yields
    - No soil
    - Less work
    - Healthier food


Disadvantages

    - Expensive
    - Needs electrisity (problem with power outages)
    - Requers constant supervision
    - Vunerable to water diseases
    - Not all plnats can be grown in Hydroponics
    - Time consuming
